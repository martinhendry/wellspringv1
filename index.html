‚öôÔ∏è How WellSpring Works

Navigate using the tabs at the top. Your core interactions happen on the Daily Log tab.

  • Daily Log: Select pillars you engaged with and your mood. Use arrows or click the date to change days. (Both Modes)
  • Save Day: Locks the day's entry, calculates XP/streak. Requires at least one pillar or mood logged. (Both Modes)
  • XP & Levels: Earn XP for saved days (more for pillars/mood logged). Level up for a sense of progression. Reach Level 100 to Prestige! (Both Modes)
  • Calendar: View past logs visually. Click on the days to jump to their log. (Both Modes)
  • Analytics: See overall stats (days logged, streak, etc.) and visualise pillar balance. (Full Mode Only)
  • Journey: Add notes/reflections. Achievements and Cycle completions appear automatically. Filter/sort your history. (Full Mode Only)
  • Achievements: Unlock milestones for streaks, XP, pillar counts, etc. (Full Mode Only)
  • Habit Planner: Create specific 'if/then' or 'habit stacking' plans to guide your actions. (Full Mode, Toggleable in Settings, currently in Beta)
  • Simple/Full Mode: Choose your preferred experience in Settings. Simple Mode hides Planner, Analytics, and Achievements tabs. (Both Modes)

üîí Your Data & Privacy

Your privacy is a top priority at WellSpring. We want you to feel secure and in control of your information.

Core Data Storage & Control: Only On Your Device

  • Local Storage: All your personal tracking data ‚Äì including your daily pillar logs, mood entries, notes on the Journey timeline, achievement progress, and settings ‚Äì is stored exclusively in your browser's local storage on your device.
  • No Server-Side Storage: This data is not sent to, collected by, or stored on any external servers or databases managed by WellSpring. We do not have a backend system for your personal tracking data.
  • You're in Control (Backup & Restore): You have full control over your data. Use the buttons below to download a backup file (JSON format) of all your information at any time. You can also import this data back into the app on any device. Warning: Restoring will overwrite current data.
  • Browser Specificity: Please note that your data is saved to the specific browser you are using. To access your saved data across different devices (like desktop and mobile), you would need to use the export/import feature.

Usage Analytics with Google Analytics 4 (GA4)

To help us understand how WellSpring is used and to improve its features and user experience, we use Google Analytics 4 (GA4). Here‚Äôs what this means for you:

  • Purpose of Analytics: GA4 helps us see which features are popular, how users navigate the app, identify areas for improvement, and understand general usage trends (e.g., how many people use the app, general geographic regions of users).
  • Aggregated & Anonymized Data: The information sent to GA4 is aggregated and helps us understand overall patterns. By default, GA4 is designed to not log or store full IP addresses.
  • What is NOT Tracked by GA4:
    • The specific text content of your notes on the Journey timeline.
    • Personally identifiable details from your pillar logs or mood entries.
    • Your name, if you choose to provide it for personalisation.
  • What MAY Be Tracked by GA4 (General Usage Data):
    • Events like which tabs are viewed, when a day is saved, when a note is added (but not the content), or when settings are changed.
    • General device characteristics (e.g., browser type, device category like desktop/mobile).
    • Approximate geographic location (derived from IP address, but IP addresses themselves are not stored by GA4).
    • App version and interaction with UI elements (e.g., button clicks for specific features).
  • Google's Privacy Policy: For more information on how Google uses data when you use sites or apps that use Google Analytics, please visit Google's Privacy & Terms.
